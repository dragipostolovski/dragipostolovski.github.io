About me

I am social and moderately hard-working. My versatile demeanor makes it easy for me to work both in groups and independently. I am not excessively competitive and believe there are more important things than winning. While admirable, I am aware that this can create difficulty if I find myself working with aggressive individuals.

What is my social style?

Warm-hearted and considerate, I am an all-around nice person. I enjoy team projects, but may be hesitant to step up and take the credit I deserve.

How competitive am I?

For me, life/work balance is key. I work hard, but also realize that there's no point in getting ahead if I can't relax enough to enjoy it.

How do I handle my emotions?

I am cool, calm and collected. I have a steady, comfortable demeanor and don't give in easily to moodswings or outside influences.
